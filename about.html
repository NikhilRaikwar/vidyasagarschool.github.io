About Us

Director's Message

A very warm welcome to Vidyasagar Sr. Sec. School. Vidyasagar Sr. Sec. School believes in the all-round development of an individual within a co-educational, compassionate, welcoming, and broad-minded community. We strive to create a safe environment in which students realize their greatest intellectual, creative, ethical, and physical promise. We are defined by an energized, attentive, and diverse student-teacher culture. At Vidyasagar Sr. Sec. School, we value each individual and work tirelessly to help our students achieve their highest potential. Our school programs comprise a challenging curriculum where lessons often result in the realization that there is never one way to answer a problem. While at Vidyasagar Sr. Sec. School, your child will develop an ability to thrive in a dynamic setting, the capacity to advocate for themselves and others, and cultivate an ethic of hard work and critical thinking. I encourage you to explore our programs online, but most importantly, to take time to visit us on campus. We are looking forward to meeting you.

Vidyasagar Sr. Sec. School stands tall among the prestigious educational institutions of Burhar Distt. Sahdol, celebrated for delivering unparalleled State Board education. At Vidyasagar, believe that education should lead the students to a practical, confident and successful living.
